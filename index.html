Venturi Flush

Fresh water availability is a major environmental problem in several areas of the world and will become a global problem soon. It is also foolish & criminal on our part to continue to flush billions of gallons of treated fresh water down our toilets everyday just to clear our excreta. Since 40% of the 6 billion people on earth use toilets, it accounts to a waste of lot of water.

Here we present Venturi Flush. A redesign of the water closet to reduce the consumption of water, currently at 6 to 12 liters per flush to just 3 liters per flush. A simple mechanism added to the conventional closet that will create a partial vacuum when the user pushes down the flush lever. This vacuum will partially suck the excreta out and thereby reducing the dependence on the flushing action to remove the excreta.

GadRent

A majority of e-shoppers commonly undergo selection ambiguity problem while choosing products from numerous available gadgets. The customers are not sure if the product will satisfy their needs completely. It is difficult for the e-shoppers to choose the right product before buying just by relying on the reviews. Though return/replacement procedures for online purchases are available, it is cumbersome, time consuming and not convenient. Certain online shopping portals follow only exchange policy and no money back policy. Even if money back policy is facilitated the time required to get back the money is quite slow.

Here we present GADRENT, a one stop solution for e-shoppers to guide themselves in making wise and satisfactory choices. We are a platform where customers can try and rent electronic gadgets for certain period of time. We provide convenient try-before-you-own service for electronic gadgets such as smart-phones, cameras, laptops, wearable, Printers etc.
